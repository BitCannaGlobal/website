    BitCanna is shaping the future of cannabis culture through innovative technology.

    Our Mission

    BitCanna was initially founded to address the challenges surrounding payments within the legal cannabis industry. As these issues have begin to resolve, we're revisiting our mission to reflect our evolving focus.

    Web3 Gaming & NFTs
    With our transition to the CosmosSDK-based blockchain, we now have the infrastructure to create and accept transactions through our mobile and web wallets, as well as CosmosPay. Our next step is to expand into web3 gaming and establishing a DAO for our NFT collection, "BitCanna Buddheads".

    Web3 Development
    In addition to building a thriving community, our developers will continue to create new apps and tools for use within the Cosmos Interchain. This will enable us to collaborate with other projects and bring the benefits of these partnerships to our token and NFT holders.

    Cannabis Community
    As a pioneer in the crypto cannabis space, BitCanna has been around since 2018. Now, we're taking the next step in building a strong and vibrant community around our shared passion for cannabis and web3 innovation.

    Get Involved
    We can't do this without our community. That's why we invite all crypto cannabis enthusiasts to join us on this journey. Follow us on X, and join our Discord and Telegram channels. It's going to be an exciting time!
